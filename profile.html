NAGA Terms and Conditions

Terms and Conditions
The garden plot(s) are for the sole use of the gardener and his or her immediate family only. It cannot be assigned or transferred.

This permit is valid from May 15, or after the plots have been tilled and staked, until Oct. 31.

Crops, plant supports, string and all personal implements must be removed by Oct. 31

Members must cooperate with the Maintenance Director, especially during clean up.

Members must pull weeds regularly and control plant disease and insect infestation.

Plots are to be kept neat and tidy and the long grass on the perimeters is to be trimmed.

Non-plant refuse is to be placed in garbage receptacles. Compost bins are provided for plant refuse except corn stalks and sunflowers.

Corn stalks and Sunflowers are to be cut into six inch pieces and tilled into your plot at the end of the season. DO NOT PLACE IN GARBAGE OR IN COMPOST BINS.

Watering may be done only by hand.

Use of DDT, ALL HERBICIDES, FUNGICIDES, AND INSECTICIDES, EXCEPT ORGANIC SUBSTANCES, IS FORBIDDEN.

Produce is not to be sold, and any violation will result in the loss of your garden.

Paths, laneways and the parking lot must be kept free and clear of obstacles and vegetation.

Rocks are not to be thrown in the paths or laneways.

All terms and conditions of the license will be strictly enforced. This permit may be withdrawn by the Association at any time or not renewed should the member fail to use the garden plot in a good and proper manner, be guilty of improper conduct or interfere with other members’ garden plots.

Any dispute arising from the use or occupation of a garden plot or locker shall be settled by the directors or a person designated by them.

The Association shall incur no liability for loss, damage or injury sustained by the members or their family groups.

As part of NAGA’s lease agreement with the City of Ottawa, no gardeners should ever park their vehicles in the Fire Station parking lot on Viewmount Drive.

Should a NAGA members' vehicle ever be found parked at the Fire Station parking lot, the membership will be immediately cancelled with NAGA without any prior notifications.
